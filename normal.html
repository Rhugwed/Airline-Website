Special Baggage



Sports/Music/Other Equipments

Golfing Equipment

  • Kits containing only 1 Golf bag and a pair of shoes will be charged equal to 6 Kilos of excess baggage per kit.
  • Only 1 such kit may be pooled in the Free Baggage Allowance of the class of service used.

Angling Kit

  • The Angling Kit shall be treated to be equal to 4 Kilos of excess baggage.
  • Such kit may be pooled with the Free Baggage Allowance and if in excess of baggage allowance due to this inclusion, shall be charged as above.

Snow/Water Skiing Equipment

  • The skiing equipment kit may contain 1 pair of skis, one pair of boots or 1 snowboard, 1 pair of boots and 1 pair of standard water skis.
  • Charges shall be at the applicable rate for 3 Kilos of excess baggage.
